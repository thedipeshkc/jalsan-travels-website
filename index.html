About Us

Gothatar's Trusted
Travel & Finance Hub

Jalsan Travels & Tours P. Ltd. was established in 2020 and is located in Gothatar-7, Kathmandu (Kageshwori Manahara area), conveniently situated on the way to Tejbinayak, near Birendrachok,Forcepark. We are a proud member of NATTA.

Led by Managing Director Mr. Hari Bdr. Karki, our experienced team provides fast, reliable, and friendly service. We serve local residents of Gothatar and the wider Kathmandu valley — as well as Nepali diaspora families who rely on us to ensure remittances reach their loved ones safely and quickly.
